# 📊 Olist E-Commerce Sales & Delivery Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Power BI dashboard built using the Olist e-commerce dataset. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights for business decision-making.

## 🎯 Business Problems
1. What drives revenue and how can it be improved?
2. How efficient is delivery and how does it impact customer satisfaction?

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated in a few top product categories.
- Credit cards dominate customer payment methods.
- A noticeable revenue drop occurs around September, indicating potential seasonality or operational issues.
- Most deliveries are on time or early, but delays significantly reduce customer ratings.
- Delivery performance varies across regions, highlighting logistics inefficiencies.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Page 1: Business Performance & Revenue Drivers
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top Product Categories by Revenue
- Revenue Distribution by Payment Method
- Top States by Order Volume
- KPIs: Total Revenue, Total Orders, Avg Order Value, Top Category

### 🔹 Page 2: Delivery & Customer Experience
- Expected vs Actual Delivery Trend
- On-Time vs Late Deliveries
- Impact of Delivery Performance on Customer Ratings
- KPIs: Avg Delivery Days, On-Time %, Late %, Avg Rating

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Data Modeling

## 📂 Dataset
Olist E-Commerce Dataset (Brazil)

## 🚀 Outcome
The dashboard provides a clear view of business performance, identifies revenue drivers, and highlights operational areas for improvement in delivery and customer experience.
